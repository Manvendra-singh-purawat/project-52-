Interesting Facts About Camels:

  • There are two types of camels: One humped or “dromedary” camels and two humped Bactrian camels.
  • Camels have three sets of eyelids and two rows of eyelashes to keep sand out of their eyes.
  • Camels have thick lips which let them forage for thorny plants other animals can't eat.

Interesting Facts About Chameleons:

  • Most chameleons have a prehensile tail that they use to wrap around tree branches.
  • Chameleons live in Madagascar, Africa, Spain, Portugal and Asia in rain forests, savannas.
  • According to the Integrated Taxonomic Information System (ITIS), there are 171 species of chameleons.

Interesting Facts About Cicadas:

  • . Cicadas are known for their singing, and their violin shaped body structure
  • Cicadas have the unique ability to turn themselves into evaporative coolers
  • If you have ever tried to locate a cicada then you already know it’s not easy to catch them

Interesting Facts About Panda:

  • Giant Pandas are good at climbing trees and can also swim.
  • An adult can eat 12–38 kilos of bamboo per day!
  • Pandas have carnivorous teeth, but they eat bamboo and fruit.

Interesting Facts About Pigeons:

  • They Can Recognize Themselves.
  • Pigeons are Very Fast.
  • They are Incredibly Social.
